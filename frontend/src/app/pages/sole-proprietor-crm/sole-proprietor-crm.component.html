Sole Proprietor CRM

Sole Proprietor CRM is a streamlined, efficient, and self-hosted Customer Relationship Management(CRM) tailored specifically for sole proprietors and freelancers.

Unlike bloated enterprise solutions, this CRM focuses on the essentials: organizing client interactions, tracking leads, managing invoices, and generating reports—without subscription fees or vendor lock-in.

Built with PHP and MySQL, it emphasises simplicity, security, and full data ownership.

Project Purpose and Goal

My goal was to build a functional CRM that removes the friction between lead capture and revenue. I wanted a system that:

  • Keeps client data centralized and secure
  • Automates invoice generation and tracking
  • Offers a self-hosted, cost-effective alternative to SaaS tools

The result is a lightweight platform that manages the full client lifecycle—from first contact to closed deal.
